Purpose of the Study:
This study aims to analyze brainwave signals using the NeuroSky MindLink Mobile 2 to develop an adaptive learning model that can improve student focus and performance. The signals will be processed using STFT and Kalman filters to classify different cognitive states.

Procedures:
As a participant, you will be asked to:

  • Wear the NeuroSky headset during study sessions (05-10 minutes).
  • Participate in basic learning/focus activities.
  • Allow EEG data collection for research use.

Voluntary Participation:
Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Confidentiality:
Your data will be anonymized and stored securely. No identifying info will be included in publications.

Risks and Discomforts:
